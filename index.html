Group Beginner Lesson

3 Hours

Lesson for players brand new to the game! We will go over the history, the tiles, and the rules that make this game addictive. This is a 3-hour lesson with 2 guided play games to understand play. That includes: how to use the NMJL card, how to deal, the Charleston, and of course how to work towards Mahjong! Recommended for a group of 4–8 people. Private lessons are also available. This lesson includes a 2026 NMJL card* for you to keep ($14/pp value).
